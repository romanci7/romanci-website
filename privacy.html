Summary

Short version: we collect minimal data needed to deliver services. On this demo site some data is stored locally (in your browser).

Why this policy exists

overview

Romanci values your privacy. This policy explains the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you. It covers website visitors, clients, and contributors.

Scope

who & what

This policy applies to information collected on our website, via forms (contact, vouch, quote), and during service delivery. It does not govern third-party sites; see the “Sharing & Third-parties” section below.

What information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, automatically-generated technical data, and (where applicable) third-party data.

Information you provide

forms, messages

Examples: your name, email, phone number, project details, vouches/testimonials, and attachments you upload. For account recovery we may request historical account details to prove ownership (never share passwords via insecure channels).

Automatically collected data

logs & analytics

When you visit the site we may record request logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps), basic analytics (pages visited, referrer) to improve reliability. On the demo site these logs are transient and primarily browser-side or server logs from your local server.

Third-party data

integrations

When you choose third-party services (payment processors, hosting, analytics), those providers may collect data. We recommend checking their privacy policies. We don’t sell your personal data.

How we use your data

Deliver services

primary purpose

We use collected information to provide services: account recovery, Discord setup, website development, and customer support. This includes contacting you, preparing appeals, and implementing site changes.

Analytics & improvements

improve

We may analyse usage patterns to improve the website, user flows, and reliability. On the demo site analytics are minimal and often simulated for testing.

Legal & safety

compliance

We may retain and process data to comply with legal obligations, address abuse, or protect the rights and safety of others.

Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. Limited sharing occurs with trusted service providers only when necessary to deliver a service.

Service providers

examples

Examples: hosting providers, payment processors, analytics vendors, and collaboration tools. We require these providers to follow appropriate security measures.

Legal requests

law enforcement

Where required by law or to protect rights, we may disclose information to comply with legal processes (e.g., court orders) or to defend against claims.

Cookies & Tracking

Cookies help keep sessions, preferences, and analytics. You can manage or block cookies in your browser.

Types of cookies

necessary, analytics

We may use essential cookies for site function and optional cookies for analytics. On this demo site analytics are optional and lightweight.

Control

your choice

To change cookie preferences use your browser settings or privacy tools. Blocking cookies may affect site functionality.

Your rights & choices

You have rights to access, correct, export, and request deletion of your personal data. Below are practical options for this demo site.

Request access / export

download

You can export your data (vouches, contact messages, quotes) using the Data Tools on the right. For production services we'll provide a formal request channel via email or dashboard.

Request deletion

erase

On this demo site you can remove local demo data using the Data Tools. For real projects we will accept verified deletion requests via WhatsApp or email — see the Contact section.

How to escalate

support

If you are unhappy with our response, contact us at the WhatsApp number or Instagram handle and we will escalate your request internally.
